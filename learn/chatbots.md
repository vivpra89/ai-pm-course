# Chatbots & UX — memory, tools, safe flows

*Estimated study: 4–6 hours · aligns with Weeks 9–10.*

---

## Outcomes

- Design **conversational state** (session vs long-term memory — privacy tradeoffs).  
- Wire **tools** (SQL, tickets, search) with clear success/failure UX.  
- **Streaming**, latency budgets, and **empty states** for enterprise users.
